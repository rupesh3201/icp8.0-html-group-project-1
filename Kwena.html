Kwena Maphaka

                                                                                                                                 

Kwena Tshegofatso Maphaka (born 8 April 2006) is a South African cricketer who plays age-group cricket representing South Africa national under-19 cricket team. He plays for the Mumbai Indians in the IPL and Paarl Royals in the SA20.[1] He made his IPL Debut against Sunrisers Hyderabad.
