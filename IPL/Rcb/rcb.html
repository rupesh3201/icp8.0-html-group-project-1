RCB (royal challengers bangolore)

        Royal Challengers Bengaluru, formerly Royal Challengers Bangalore, are a professional franchise cricket team based in Bengaluru, Karnataka, that plays in the Indian Premier League. The franchise was founded in 2008 by United Spirits and was named after its liquor brand Royal Challenge.
